About

My name is Stephen Adams. I am currently finishing my PhD in computing from the University of Kent. My thesis is titled “Data-Driven Refactorings for Haskell” wherein I describe the development of “data-driven” refactorings. These refactorings help evolve the data types of Haskell programs. The latex version of my thesis can be found on GitHub: https://github.com/SAdams601/thesis and the branch of HaRe (the Haskell Refactorer) where most of my work is also on GitHub https://github.com:SAdams601/HaRe.git.

This website is my personal blog.
